    About Academic Tracker

    Academic Tracker helps you manage your courses, track your academic progress by semester, and visualize your achievements. Add, edit, and organize your courses, and export or import your data for backup or transfer. All data is stored locally in your browser for privacy and speed.

    How to Use

    • Add your courses with details like name, code, semester, credits, and status.
    • Track your progress per semester and overall.
    • Export your data for backup, or import it to restore progress.
    • Use search and filters to quickly find courses.
    • All features work offline and data is private to your device.
